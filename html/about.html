About Us

OhNoDistrollo is devoted to providing thoughtful takes on videogame design. We offer analyses of various game elements, including gameplay mechanics, systems, storytelling, graphics, and more, but we will focus on what lies behind our surface-level feelings about these elements.

By engaging in this introspection, we hope to foster a deeper understanding and appreciation for games, learn more about how to communicate about game elements, and set an example for how to be a more discerning gamer.

OhNoDistrollo aims to be a valuable resource for the gamer seeking a fresh perspective or the developer aspiring to improve player enjoyment.
